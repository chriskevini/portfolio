Sir Hires

サー・ハイアーズ

The job hunt is an exercise in tedium. Sir Hires rewrites that narrative with a privacy-first extension that extracts, organises, and enhances job data — powered entirely by a local LLM. No servers. No surveillance. No compromise.

“No data ever leaves your device.”

Iterature

イテラチャー

A writing coach that meets you where you are. Iterature adapts to your rhythm — ten quiet minutes or a full half-hour of flow. AI-powered critique arrives when you're ready, not before. For solo creators who want to improve without judgement.

“Practice your craft in a low-pressure,
constructive environment.”

WordFractal

ワード・フラクタル

Language as landscape. WordFractal drops players onto a shared letter grid and asks them to build — together — chains of words that reach toward the horizon. A race with vocabulary as fuel. Supports English and Japanese dictionaries.

“Collaboratively build chains of words
across an expansive grid.”

NCHI Slider

んちスライダー

The familiar mechanics of 2048, reimagined through Japanese wordplay. Slide hiragana tiles, combine them into words, chase your name up the global leaderboard. Deceptively simple. Quietly addictive. Fully bilingual in spirit.

“A playful collision of puzzle
mechanics and language.”
